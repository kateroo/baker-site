OUR SAFETY COMMITMENT

At BakerCorp, we know that safety doesn’t start and stop at your jobsite. We’re committed to keeping everyone safe around the clock, and that includes protecting the health and safety of our people, our customers and communities where we operate. We pledge not to compromise the safety of our workers to achieve any operational or business objectives. Our goal is to prevent all accidents and occupational injuries.

SAFETY IS
OUR #1 PRIORITY

BakerCorp employees are trained on safe operating procedures,  have stop-work authority for all our equipment and understand that good customer service never means pushing the limits of an application setup if safety might be jeopardized.  They are also expected to participate in the continuous improvement of work conditions and practices to reduce hazards in the workplace.BakerCorp recognizes that safety doesn’t start and stop on your job site; it’s a commitment to a way of doing business 24/7/365. Below are some of the key ways we’re keeping safety top of mind across the company.

Working Smart

ON & OFF JOB SITE
  • Driver Awareness
  • PPE Compliance: Assessing correct PPE, tools, and equipment
  • Environmental Awareness: Keeping clean and hazard free work areas, vehicles and equipment
  • Daily Job Safety Analysis (JSAs): Reducing risks in the workplace
  • Stop Work Authority: Stopping any task if there is a threat to employees, equipment, property and/or the environment
  • Green Hat Short Service Employee “SSE” Program
  • Behavior Based Safety Observation Program
  • Near Miss Reporting Program
  • 5S Program (Green Belt)
OFF JOB SITE
  • Safety business plan specific to each customer job
  • Monthly CEO safety performance updates
  • Safety topics open each meeting
